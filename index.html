About LUWI FOUNDATION Zambia

Who We Are: We support students and pupils in need — including intersex and disabled students. Our programs address trauma, abuse, exploitation, mental health, GBV, and financial hardship.

Mission: Empower students and pupils affected by trauma, abuse, exploitation, GBV, mental health challenges, and financial hardship.

Vision: A Zambia where every student and pupil has access to education, protection, and hope.

Founder: Bonafide Mulunga — committed to student protection and youth empowerment.

Our Programs

Education Support

Scholarships, school supplies, mentorship, and support for intersex and disabled students.

Community Awareness

Health, GBV prevention, mental health education, and social awareness campaigns for students and pupils.

Student Protection & Outreach

Support for students and pupils facing abuse, exploitation, trauma, and emotional challenges.
