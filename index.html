About LUWI FOUNDATION Zambia

Who We Are: LUWI FOUNDATION Zambia supports students and pupils in need — including intersex and disabled children. We address trauma, abuse, exploitation, mental health, GBV, financial hardship, and more.

Our Mission: To empower children affected by trauma, abuse, exploitation, GBV, mental health challenges, and financial hardship.

Our Vision: A Zambia where every child has access to education, protection, and hope.

Founder: Bonafide Mulunga — dedicated to child protection, community support, and youth empowerment.

Our Programs

Education Support
